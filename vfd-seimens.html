SIEMENS VFD-S120

The incredibly small and sturdy SINAMICS S120 Combi drive concept is designed specifically for small turning and milling machines. The SINAMICS S120 Combi combines a TTL encoder interface, power units for spindle and feed motors, and a line infeed with regenerative feedback capability into a single Power Module.
In machine tools, the SINAMICS S120 Combi Power Modules are best suited as drives for three to six axes. Frame types A01 through A07, as well as B01 and B02 with external air cooling, are available for the Power Modules. The expansion axes are booksize small SINAMICS Motor Modules.
